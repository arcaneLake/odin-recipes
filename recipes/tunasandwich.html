Tuna Sandwich

About this recipe

Here is my favorite easy-to-make sandwich!

Ingredients

  • Tuna
  • Mayo
  • Salt and pepper
  • Green peppers
  • Onions
  • Thick toast

Steps

  1. Open and drain tuna can.
  2. Mix in tuna with mayo, season with salt and pepper.
  3. Add in diced veggies.
  4. Toast bread, and assemble.
